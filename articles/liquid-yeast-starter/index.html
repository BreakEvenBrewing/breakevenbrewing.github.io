    Liquid Yeast Starter

    How to size and make a 2L yeast starter. This should be done 48-72 hours before pitching.

    Equipment Needed

    1. Erlenmeyer flask and Foam Stopper
    2. Magnetic Stir-bar
    3. Stir-plate
    4. DME (pale or pilsner depending on recipe)
    5. Yeast Nutrient
    6. Yeast

    Hazards Identification and PPE / Safety Steps Required:

    PPE: Heat resistant gloves

    Hazardous Products: Heat

    Hazards: Boiling in flask

    Preparation

    1. Clean Erlenmeyer flask and Stir-bar

    Steps

    1. Add 1.5L of water to the flask
    2. Add 200g of DME
    3. Top flask with water to 2L mark
    4. Add ⅛ teaspoon of preferred yeast nutrient
    5. Cover opening with foil and boil for 15min
    6. Allow the flask to cool to room temperatures and aseptically transfer yeast to liquid
    7. Swirl and place on stir plate
