    Cleaning Fermenters

    This will walk you through cleaning and sanitizing a fermenter so that it is ready for fresh wort to be transferred into it.

    Equipment Needed

    1. CIP Pump
    2. CIP Spray Ball 1.5" TC
    3. Brewery hose to connect CIP Pump to CIP Spray Ball
    4. Alkaline Brewery Wash
    5. White wash basin
    6. 2" TC straight 8" run to return to basin

    Hazards Identification and PPE / Safety Steps Required:

    PPE: Eye protection, rubber boots, gloves

    Hazardous Products: Alkaline Brewery Wash

    Hazards: Handling hazardous products

    Preparation

    1. Confirm fermenter is empty and ready to be cleaned
    2. Dump remaining contents of fermenter into bucket and dispose

    Steps

    1. Fill basin with clean water
    2. Insert CIP ball into 1.5" TC CIP port
    3. Attach 8" run to dump valve to return to basin
    4. Attach pump to CIP ball and run for 15 mins to rinse fermenter
    5. Dispose of liquid
    6. Refill basin with 5 gallons of hot tap water
    7. Mix in Akaline Brewery Wash based on packaging
    8. Run for 15 mins
    9. Rinse again with clean water
      1. If not promptly using fermenter, seal and put under positive pressure until ready for use
